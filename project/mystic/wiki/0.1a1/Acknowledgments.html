Acknowledgments

Building good software is hard. A special thanks to all of those who have contributed to the development so far.

If you use this software to do productive scientific research that leads to publication, the authors ask that you acknowledge use of the software by citing the following paper in your publication:: 

    "mystic: a simple model-independent inversion framework",
     Michael McKerns, Patrick Hung, and Michael Aivazis, unpublished;
     http://dev.danse.us/trac/mystic
